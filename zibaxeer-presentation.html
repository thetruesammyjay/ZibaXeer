Most platforms hide trade execution behind black-box systems. Followers can't verify what's happening with their capital.

Custody Risk

When a platform holds your funds, you're exposed to hacks, insolvencies, and exit scams with no recourse.

Misaligned Fees

Managers charge recurring fees even when they lose money. Their revenue is decoupled from your returns.

The Solution

ZibaXeer Makes Copy-Trading Verifiable and Non-Custodial

Leaders create strategy vaults. Followers subscribe with configurable risk bounds. All critical state lives on-chain. Fees only trigger on realized profit — aligning leader and follower incentives by design.

Transparent

Every trade, vault event, and settlement recorded on HyperPaxeer. Queryable via PaxScan.

Non-Custodial

Capital stays in auditable smart contracts. Leaders have trade authority only — never withdrawal access.

Performance-Only Fees

5–25% of realized profits. Zero management fees. Leaders win exactly when followers win.
